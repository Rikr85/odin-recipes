Recetas de Cocina: Recetas de Odin. 6 cenas rápidas y fáciles de comida reconfortante de 5 ingredientes para el otoño

El desafío:

Necesita cenas rápidas y fáciles entre semana que no requieren muchos ingredientes pero que aún así satisfagan su antojo de comida reconfortable de otoño.

La solución:

Pruebe estas abudantes recetas que están listas en 30 minutos o menos y solo necesita cinco ingredientes para prepararlas (sin contar la sal, la pimienta y el aceite). !Tu dilema de la cena está resuelto!

  • Red Curry Chicken

    Ready in 30 minutes. Boneless chicken breasts are browned and simmered in a curry-laced coconut cream sauce while you boil rice noodles to stir into the mix. You do what Alex E. did and add broccoli for color and a vegetable component.

  • Honey Mustard Pork Chops

    Ready in 30 minutes. You can follow the recipe and prepare this in a skillet, or do what SheShe1987 did and bake it. "Simple yet delish! I followed the recipe except I baked my chops in the oven on 375. My 4-year-old granddaughter ate two, and hubby was in love. I think I have found his new fav pork chop. LOL"

  • Honey Glazed Chicken

    Ready in about 20 minutes. Honey-sweetened soy sauce and a hit of red pepper flakes make up the pan sauce for this ridiculously easy dinner. KalenD says, "Super easy and tasty. Make this all the time. We stir fry some zucchini, squash, onions, and red bell peppers. Make some extra sauce to put on these veggies." Then she serves the whole thing over brown rice. So simple and good!

  • Argentinean Skirt Steaks

    Ready in 20 minutes. Recipe submitter LauraT says, "This is the most requested main dish in my house. Everyone loves it and that is a rare thing indeed! These steaks are almost too simple to believe and they turn out great every single time. So tender and flavorful - you just can't mess them up! The adobo seasoning is quite salty, so keep that in mind as you season the steaks."
