Argentinean Skirt Steaks

Description:

This is the most requested main dish in my house. Everyone loves it and that is a rare thing indeed! These steaks are almost too simple to believe and they turn out great every single time. So tender and flavorful - you just can't mess them up! The adobo seasoning is quite salty, so keep that in mind as you season the steaks.

  • Prep Time: 10 mins
  • Cook Time: 10 mins
  • Total Time: 20 mins
  • Servings: 4
  • Yield: 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds skirt steak, trimmed and cut into 2 1/2-inch wide pieces
  • 1 teaspoon adobo seasoning, or to taste
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Directions

  1. Step 1: Season skirt steak pieces all over with adobo.
  2. Step 2: Heat olive oil and butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook steak in hot oil and butter, turning often, until steak begins to firm, and is reddish-pink and juicy in the center, 6 to 8 minutes.

Cook's Note:

I usually end up popping them in the microwave on a microwaveable serving platter for another minute or two to prevent over-browning, yet assuring that they are done all the way through.

Nutrition Facts

  • 531 Calories
  • 20g Fat
  • 67g Carbs
