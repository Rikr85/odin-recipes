Honey Galzed Chicken

Description:

This honey chicken recipe was given to me by a friend. I've used this recipe for years, and my family requests it often. It's a light meal that everyone will enjoy. Serve with steamed rice.

  • Prep Time: 10 mins
  • Cook Time: 10 mins
  • Total Time: 20 mins
  • Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1/8 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into bite-size pieces

Directions

  1. Step 1: Gather all ingredients.
  2. Step 2: Whisk honey, soy sauce, and red pepper flakes in a bowl; set aside.
  3. Step 3: Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir chicken in hot oil until lightly brown, about 5 minutes.
  4. Step 4: Pour honey mixture into the skillet; continue to cook and stir until chicken is on longer pink in the center and sauce is thickened, about 5 minutes more.
  5. Step 5: Serve hot and enjoy!

Nutrition Facts

  • 179 Calories
  • 7g Fat
  • 18g Carbs
  • 13g Protein
